MADRID SOFÍA HERNÁNDEZ PITARCH 21 YEARS

Hello! My name is Sofía Hernández Pitarch, and I'm a graphic design student from Madrid. My work is based on a strong capacity for adaptability and problem-solving, qualities that allow me to approach each project with an analytical and creative perspective. I enjoy teamwork and always maintain clear and effective communication, ensuring a smooth and collaborative work.

Furthermore, I practice continuous learning, which allows me to update my knowledge and stay current with new design tools, trends, and methodologies. This portfolio brings together the most important projects from my journey as a graphic designer, in which I explain the brief and the design decisions made for each project.
